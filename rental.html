Rental Terms & Policies

Security Deposit

A refundable security deposit of 25-50% of equipment value is required. Deposit is returned upon safe return of equipment.

Damage Policy

Minor wear is expected. Accidental damage will be assessed and repair costs deducted from deposit. Insurance options available.

Late Return

Late returns incur additional daily charges. Extensions available subject to availability and advance notice.

Cancellation

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before rental. Later cancellations may incur charges based on advance payment.

Delivery Service

Equipment delivery available within city limits for additional fee. Delivery charges based on distance and equipment value.

Technical Support

Free technical support and equipment orientation included. 24/7 emergency support available for major productions.
