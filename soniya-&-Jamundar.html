Their Story

Soniya and Jamundar's love story began in college where they met during a cultural festival. Their friendship blossomed into love over the years, and after dating for five years, Sushant proposed to Mridula during a vacation in Bali. Their wedding was a beautiful blend of South Indian traditions with a modern touch, celebrating their shared heritage and love for each other.

The three-day celebration featured colorful ceremonies, joyous dancing, and heartfelt moments with friends and family. We were honored to document their special journey from the Mehndi ceremony to the grand reception.
